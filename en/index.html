Welcome to ArabR

ArabR is part of global R community

2022-01-23
ArabR aims to creat a sustainable capacity building for Arabic R users through support, networking and increasing their visibility to drive the creation of more useR/R-Ladies groups in the region. Our mission is to achieve improved representation by encouraging, inspiring, and empowering individuals from Arabic speaking countries to engage with the the global R community.
